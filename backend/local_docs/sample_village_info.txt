
VILLAGE PENSION SCHEME 2024
---------------------------

1. Eligibility:
   - Residents over 60 years of age.
   - Annual income less than 50,000 INR.
   - Must be a resident of the village for at least 5 years.

2. Benefits:
   - Monthly pension of 1,500 INR.
   - Free medical checkups at the Primary Health Centre.

3. Application Process:
   - Submit Form A-1 at the Panchayat Office.
   - Attach Aadhaar Card and Income Certificate.
   - Approval takes 15 days.

4. Grievance Redressal:
   - Meet the Sarpanch every Monday between 10 AM and 12 PM.
